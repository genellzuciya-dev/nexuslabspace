01 / What We Are
The Lab

central link.

I'm Genell Zuciya, an Innovation Strategist with nearly two decades of experience designing immersive experiences, building with emerging technology, and applying nature-inspired thinking to complex problems. Nexus Lab Space is my independent lab where those threads come together.

"Science, creativity, and community meet here to create systems-level change through hands-on prototyping."

The work is hands-on and collaborative. From Legos to lasers, we use physical materials and immersive experimentation to spark bold thinking and tangible outcomes for organizations, communities, and individuals ready to move ideas forward.

03 / Lab Focus
Innovation Areas

What we make here
matters everywhere.

Nexus Lab Space guides organizations, communities, and individuals in turning bold ideas into purposeful action shaping sustainable and regenerative futures through hands-on prototyping.

001 Toys & Games Design +

Play is one of nature's most powerful learning mechanisms. We design toys and games that teach systems thinking, build empathy, and spark creative problem solving. From card games that map complex ecosystems to physical prototypes that simulate social dynamics, every piece is designed with intention.

002 Systems Thinking Workshops +

Every challenge is a system. We facilitate workshops that help teams see the whole picture: the feedback loops, the leverage points, and the unintended consequences hiding in plain sight. Rooted in ecological and organizational theory, these sessions turn complexity into clarity and confusion into confident action.

003 Hands-On Prototyping +

Ideas need to become things. We work with organizations and individuals to move concepts from sketch to prototype using the full lab toolkit. Laser cutters, 3D printers, electronics, Lego, and everything in between. Physical making unlocks thinking that whiteboards never can.

004 Immersive Experience Innovation +

Drawing on nearly two decades of work at Disney Imagineering, Meow Wolf, and Art Processors, I bring deep expertise in designing experiences that shift perspective and leave lasting impressions. We help organizations translate their mission into moments people remember and carry with them.

005 Community & Org Strategy +

Behavior doesn't change in a vacuum. It changes when the context around it shifts. Working with communities, nonprofits, and mission-driven organizations, I help design the conditions that make new patterns of thinking and acting not just possible but inevitable. By mapping the ecosystem, surfacing hidden feedback loops, and prototyping small interventions, we create the conditions where human systems can reorganize themselves toward something better.

Our Mission

To be a nexus. A powerful central link where science, creativity, and community meet to create systems-level change through hands-on prototyping.

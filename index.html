01 / What We Are
The Lab

A nexus is a
central link.
That's us.

I'm Genell Zuciya, an Innovation Strategist with nearly two decades of experience designing immersive experiences, building with emerging technology, and applying nature-inspired thinking to complex problems. Nexus Lab Space is my independent lab where those threads come together.

"Science, creativity, and community meet here to create systems-level change through hands-on prototyping."

The work is hands-on and collaborative. From Legos to lasers, we use physical materials and immersive experimentation to spark bold thinking and tangible outcomes for organizations, communities, and individuals ready to move ideas forward.

02 / How We Work
Our Approach

Nature-inspired.
Tech-enabled.
Community-driven.

We combine nature-inspired ideas, emerging technologies, and social impact strategies to design solutions that are both innovative and practical. Every challenge is a system. Every system has a pattern. Nature has already solved most of the problems we're trying to crack.

Layer 01 Biomimicry & Nature-Inspired Design

Laser cutters, 3D printers, AR/VR rigs. We bring the full toolkit. Innovation needs materials, not just ideas.

Layer 03 Systems Thinking & Social Impact

We map connections, find leverage points, and design for second and third order effects.
